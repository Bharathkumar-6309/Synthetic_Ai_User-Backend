- Recurring themes with mention percentages (must sum to reasonable distribution)
- Sentiment breakdown (Positive, Neutral, Negative percentages summing to 100)
- "Would use this product?" score per persona (1-10) and aggregate percentage
- "Would pay?" estimate as percentage (typically lower than would-use)
- Key quotes (verbatim or lightly trimmed from actual responses)
- Actionable product suggestions ranked by priority
- A 2-3 sentence summary of what users want overall

Respond with ONLY valid JSON in this exact shape:
{{
  "would_use_pct": <int 0-100>,
  "would_pay_pct": <int 0-100>,
  "themes": [{{"theme": "<name>", "mentions_pct": <int>}}],
  "sentiment": {{"Positive": <int>, "Neutral": <int>, "Negative": <int>}},
  "key_quotes": [{{"quote": "<text>", "persona": "<name>"}}],
  "suggestions": [{{"suggestion": "<text>", "category": "<label>", "priority": "high|medium|low", "personas": ["<name>"]}}],
  "user_wants_summary": "<2-3 sentences>",
  "persona_scores": {{"<persona_id>": <float 1-10>}}
}}

Base every insight strictly on the provided feedback. Do not invent responses.
